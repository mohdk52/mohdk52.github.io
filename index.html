Corona Virus (COVID19) Report - Daywise

This dashboard provides reliable and real-time updates on the coronavirus based on data from credible sources, gets refreshed automatically after a regular interval of time, provides insights about the spread of COVID-19 and shows virus hotspots through red marks around different Continents of the world.

It provides a realistic assessment of the virus spread from each location. COVID-19 outbreak differs from country to country as seen from the data and this dashboard offers this information to its users.

Graphical analysis of data about COVID-19 on this dashboard simplifies trends and understanding movement of the virus. It also gives you variety of reports goto buttons to choose from. Scroll Down to see the complete Dashboard.

Corona Virus (COVID19) Report - Daywise

World's Latest Figures

This page gives the details of Confirmed, Active, Recovered and Deceased numbers for World vs Country of your choice (selected from the drop down list by clicking the arrow in the left middle). The numbers in the square brackets are the new additions compared to day before.

The trend lines gives you exact picture of how the Country is doing over time. Users of this dashboard benefit from different colors for infections, deaths, recovered cases and improves visibility.

Indian Premier League (IPL) Report

This Dashboard covers data for all the seasons from 2008 to 2019. Current season is covered in a separate page and has quick real-time statistics around it.

The Dashboard has analysis for Batting team as well as Bowling team with an additional layer of analysis done at player level. It also allows user to select teams and shows the match summary for the matches played between the selected 2 teams.

Indian Premier League (IPL) Report

Overall Data Analysis

This page allows user to select a season and it displays Winner/RunnerUp Teams, Player of the series, Total matches/wickets/runs scored, Total 4's/6's as KPI's.

And has variety of useful visual for data analysis by blending data to show Top 5 Batsman/Bowler, Venue details, Winning share of a team and Points table.
